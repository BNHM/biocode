A Collecting Event is a convenient way to describe the collection of one or more specimens at a distinct locality and time, by a particular collector(s). The specimens collected in a collecting event may or may not have been identified and catalogued.

Use this form to query Moorea Biocode's database of collecting events. Type all or part of a word, case unimportant, or choose a word from a drop-down list. If more than one field is completed, the search will look for records that meet both conditions.
